About DeVoll

Sixty Years of
Precision Craft

We're not a portal. We're not a marketplace. We're a shop — family-owned, in Victorville, California, where skilled machinists have been turning raw rubber into precision components since 1964.

DeVoll Rubber Mfg Group is a domestic manufacturer of custom rubber products serving industrial, commercial, and specialty markets across the United States. Every part we make is engineered to your exact specification — no off-the-shelf compromises.

Our team combines decades of hands-on expertise with modern CNC waterjet and lathe capabilities. Whether you need a single prototype gasket or a full production run of complex molded components, you work directly with the people cutting your parts.

Polymer Database

Material
Compounds

Full Polymer Guide ▸
NR
Natural Rubber

Exceptional elasticity, resilience, and tensile strength. Ideal for vibration isolation, shock absorbers, and dynamic applications requiring high elongation.

CR
Neoprene

Outstanding weather, ozone, and flame resistance with good mechanical properties. Widely used in outdoor sealing, electrical insulation, and marine environments.

EPDM
EPDM

Superior UV, ozone, heat, and steam resistance. The workhorse for roofing, automotive weatherstripping, and water/steam sealing up to 300°F.

NBR
Nitrile

Excellent resistance to petroleum-based oils, fuels, and hydraulic fluids. Essential for oil seals, fuel system components, and hydraulic applications.

VMQ
Silicone

Extreme temperature range (−80°F to +450°F), outstanding biocompatibility and inertness. Used in food processing, medical devices, aerospace, and high-temp sealing.

FKM
Viton® (FKM)

Industry-leading resistance to fuels, solvents, chemicals, and high heat (up to 400°F continuous). The premium choice for critical chemical and aerospace sealing.

SBR
SBR

Cost-effective general-purpose compound. Good abrasion resistance and aging properties for wear pads, conveyor components, and sheet stock applications.

IIR
Butyl (IIR)

Exceptional impermeability to gases and moisture. Used in inner tubes, air barriers, chemical tank linings, and vibration damping applications.

Facility Systems

Equipment &
Infrastructure

🔄
CNC Lathe Systems

Multiple CNC and manual lathes capable of cutting gaskets, washers, rings, and complex rotational forms from sheet, rod, or tube stock in any rubber compound. Capacity from miniature o-rings to large-diameter industrial parts.

💧
Waterjet Cutting Cell

High-pressure abrasive waterjet system for complex profiles, nested layouts, and thick-section cutting without heat-affected zones. Suitable for rubber, cork, fiberglass, composites, and laminated materials.

🏗️
Compression & Transfer Molding

Hydraulic molding presses for compression and transfer molding of custom rubber parts. Multi-cavity tooling available for volume efficiency. We can mold to your existing tooling or assist with tool design.

📏
Die Cutting & Fabrication

Steel-rule and rotary die cutting for gaskets and flat seals from sheet goods. Slitting, skiving, buffing, and secondary fabrication operations performed in-house for complete part readiness.

🔩
Extrusion Lines

Custom rubber extrusion for cords, tubing, seals, and complex profiles in continuous or cut lengths. Multiple durometers and compounds. Vulcanization and post-cure performed on-site.

🎯
Roller Covering Shop

Full-service rubber roller covering, grinding, and reconditioning for industrial applications. Bonding, sleeve fitting, and precision OD grinding to specification. Re-covering of existing rollers available.
