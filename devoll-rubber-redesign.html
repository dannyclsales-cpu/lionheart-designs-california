Our Story

The Classic
American Story.

"Founded in a garage. Grown by family. Built on precision."

DeVoll Rubber was founded in 1964 by Millard DeVoll in Victorville, California — in his garage. Over six decades later, the company is still family-owned and operated, with Millard's son John serving as president and multiple family members contributing daily to every order that ships.

What started as a one-man operation has grown into a full-service precision rubber manufacturer serving aerospace, industrial, agricultural, and OEM markets across the country. The same commitment to quality and personal service that built the company is still the foundation today.

Polymers

Materials for
Every Application

Full Polymer Guide
NR / SBR
Natural & Synthetic Rubber
Excellent resilience and tear resistance. Ideal for mechanical goods, vibration dampening, and general-purpose applications.
CR
Neoprene
Outstanding weather, ozone, and flame resistance. Popular for outdoor seals, hose covers, and marine applications.
EPDM
EPDM
Superior UV, ozone, and steam resistance. The go-to material for automotive weatherstripping, roofing, and HVAC seals.
NBR
Nitrile (Buna-N)
Excellent resistance to petroleum-based oils and fuels. Standard choice for hydraulic seals, gaskets, and O-rings.
VMQ
Silicone
Wide temperature range (-60°F to +400°F). Clean, flexible, and FDA-compliant grades available for food and medical uses.
FKM
Viton® / Fluorocarbon
Premium resistance to chemicals, heat, and fuels. Used in aerospace, chemical processing, and demanding industrial sealing.
SBR
Styrene-Butadiene
Cost-effective general purpose rubber with good abrasion and impact resistance. Common in industrial flooring and conveyor applications.
NEOPRENE+
Specialty Blends
Custom compound development for unique application requirements. Our mixing mill lets us formulate to your exact performance needs.
