- Bear Paw Off-Road is a partner to feature prominently (they're the club's recommended mobile mechanic)
- The club is diverse: first-timers to serious crawlers, young to old, men and women — site should feel welcoming not just bro-y
- Color palette should pull from the photos: dusty desert tans, deep blacks, amber/orange light accents, sky blue accents possible
